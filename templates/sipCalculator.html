What is a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP)?

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a method of investing a fixed sum regularly in mutual funds. It allows investors to accumulate wealth over time through disciplined investing.

Benefits of SIP

SIP offers several benefits, including the ability to invest small amounts regularly, rupee cost averaging, and the potential for higher returns over the long term compared to traditional savings methods.
